Why we are here?

Our main purpose is to empower Entrepreneurs through a series of programs, workshops,
trainings, marketing, promotion, and advocacy efforts. Our Startup Hub produces a quarterly
Panorama publication, whether you’re looking for funding opportunities, incubator and accelerator
data or the government’s viewpoint on entrepreneurship in the UAE, the publication aims to simplify
and shorten your time in accessing and understanding the global market.
